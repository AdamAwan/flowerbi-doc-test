# Hey Duggee

**Hey Duggee** is a British animated children's television series created by Grant Orchard and produced by Studio AKA. It first aired on CBeebies in the UK in 2014. The show follows Duggee, a friendly big dog who runs the Squirrel Club, a after-school club for young animals. Each episode features the Squirrels – Tag, Norrie, Roly, Happy, and Betty – as they learn new skills and earn badges under Duggee's guidance.

## Premise
Duggee is a large, gentle dog who leads a group of young animal characters in various adventures. The show emphasizes problem-solving, teamwork, and positive values. Episodes typically involve the Squirrels encountering a question or problem, which Duggee helps them solve through exploration and learning, culminating in the awarding of a badge.

## Characters
- **Duggee**: The club leader; a big, friendly dog who loves hugs and helping others.
- **Tag**: A cheerful and energetic rabbit.
- **Norrie**: A sensible and kind mouse.
- **Roly**: A hyperactive and enthusiastic hippo.
- **Happy**: A calm and caring crocodile.
- **Betty**: A clever and creative octopus.

## Production
The series is known for its distinctive animation style, combining traditional 2D with stop-motion textures. It has received critical acclaim for its educational content and engaging storytelling. The show has won multiple awards, including BAFTA Children's Awards for Best Preschool Animation.

## Availability
*Hey Duggee* airs on CBeebies and is available on BBC iPlayer, as well as various streaming platforms internationally. A feature-length film, *Hey Duggee: The Badge Badge*, was released in 2022.
