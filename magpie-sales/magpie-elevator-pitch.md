# Magpie Elevator Pitch

Magpie is a Git-backed Markdown knowledge maintenance system. It indexes documentation, answers questions with citations, records weak answers to detect knowledge gaps, and automatically drafts Markdown improvements that can be published as pull requests for review. Designed to be provider-neutral, it works with OpenAI-compatible, Azure OpenAI, Codex, or Claude, and follows the principle of 'won't lie, won't leak, won't rot'.
